Bottom of the River

Songwriters:

  • Eric Hölljes
  • Ian Hölljes

Song Information:

One of Delta Rae’s best-performing and most well-known songs to date.

The lyrics illustrate the old, and often dangerous belief that some childhood illnesses were caused by possession by evil spirits or the devil,while the music video depicts a woman being led away in chains, perhaps evoking the Salem witch trials.
