Mike McKee

Mike is a drummer, and while he was not one of the original 4 members he is an integral part of Delta Rae.

Mike is a serious improvisational musician. He made his first drum kit out of various household items at age 12. To top that he recorded Delta Rae's debut album Carry the Fire using unconventional percussion instruments, including trash cans, metal chains and various mallets.

In 2010 he married Melinda Pearce.
