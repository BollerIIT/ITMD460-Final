Ian Hölljes

Ian is one of the four original members of Delta Rae and older sibling to Brittany and Eric. He has composed a number of songs for the band and also picks up vocals and guitar!

Before Delta Rae Ian attended Duke University, where he played in a band called Running Lights.

In 2014 he married Rebecca Frances Friedman, and in 2017 they had the first Delta Bae as they call him. Leo Richman Hölljes was born on Oct. 6, 2017 in Raleigh, and is pictured below.
